JR NTR

EARLY LIFE AND CAREER

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., popularly known as Jr. NTR, is an Indian film actor, Kuchipudi dancer, playback singer and television personality known for his works in Telugu cinema. He is the grandson of Telugu actor, and former chief minister of Andhra Pradesh. NTR starred his acting career as a child artist in (1996), and won the National Film Award for Best Children's Film for that year. He debuted as an adult in 2000 with the film Ninnu Choodalani. He has has worked in around twenty six films and has won one state award, two Filmfare Best Telugu Actor Award and four CineMAA Awards so far. He his also a member of his grandfather's political party, the Telugu Desam Party. Jr. NTR made his Television debut as the host of the Bigg Boss Telugu that is being broadcast on Star Maa channel. The show began airing on Star MAA from July 16, 2017.
